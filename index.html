The Sharmardiaries Band

The Sharmanaires Big Band is local band from Birmingham. They formed in the 1970s as a tribute band to the great artists of the swing era such as Glen Miller, Benny Goodman, Ted Heath, Count Basie and many more.

The Sharmanaires make regular appearances for the Solihull Royal British Legion every Thursday at Broomfield Hall. They are also available for Dinner Dances, Concerts, Weddings etc.
